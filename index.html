J. Reeko

Artist and Audio Engineer at Blueprint Sound Management

Born in Bronx, NY, Joseph Boamah, also known by his stage name J. Reeko, is a artist, audio engineer, arranger and producer by day and a tech guru by night. He was raised in the community projects of Toronto, Canada and eventually moved to Columbus, OH to further his education and career.
